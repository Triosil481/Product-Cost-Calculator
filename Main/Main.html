HBY Price Calculator

The below will allow you to calculate the price of HBY products based on the recipe and the costs of the resources used to make them

The Costs will be split into Three:

  • Resource Cost: The cost of the raw materials used to make the product
  • Labor Cost: The cost of the time spent making the product
  • Overhead Cost: The cost of utilities and other expenses related to making the product
